Part strategy, part coordination, and part digital problem-solving.

How I Work

My approach bridges the gap between administrative chaos and operational clarity. I don't just complete tasks; I build systems.
Step 01

Audit & Analyze

I start by auditing current workflows to find bottlenecks. I identify exactly where time is lost and where processes break down.

Step 02

Strategy & Plan

I design a tailored solution—whether it's a new C-Suite filing protocol, a travel logistics system, or a data tracking method.

Step 03

Build & Automate

I implement the solution using tools like Excel, Apps Script, and automation platforms to eliminate manual work and ensure accuracy.

Step 04

Execute & Train

I deploy the system and train your team (leveraging my teaching background) to ensure seamless adoption and long-term success.
